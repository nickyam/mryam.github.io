A sudden eye disease

Jan 18 2020 World 3 minutes read (About 405 words)

At 9 o’clock last night, my right eye burst into tears when I was on the Internet, and then my eyelids could not be opened. This is an eye disease I have never experienced in my life; of course, except for myopia. After ten minutes of rest, I still felt not well. I finally standed my eyelids to write a diary half-blindly, and hoped that when I fell asleep, I could heal myself.

I woke up early today and found that my symptoms did not ease. In the mirror, I found that there are some red filaments in the right eye. Just like the suspected conjunctivitis which is also known as “red eye disease”, but the symptoms of conjunctivitis are not obvious. As a person with a good attendance record, I went for work as usual. Before I start my work, I did the following things:

  • Dimmed the computer screen brightness and adjusted the warm tone to make the light softer.

  • Drinking two bags of Qingkailing granules, clearing heat and reducing fire.

  • Wipe the eyes with a paper towel to prevent tears from infecting the left eye.

  • Take a full two-hour lunch break at noon.

In the afternoon, although the red silk still exists and the right eye still has a certain bad feeling, but the symptoms gradually eased.

Looking back on this incident, I think that the unknown vomit on the bus last night could be the reason that my eye was infected or allergic to the bacteria and viruses in it.

If you encounter a similar situation in the future, you must get out of the bus immediately to avoid it, especially if the new coronavirus has recently spread.

Secondly, from my previous experience, I believe more in my ability to heal my self. Conjunctivitis usually heals itself in about 7 days (although I can’t determine if I am bearing a conjunctivitis). But when it comes to health issues, maintaining a good attitude is important.

Third, tears are good things, they can be sterilized. If I didn’t have a lot of tears on my eyes this time, I might need to buy eye drops. Although I haven’t used eye drops for more than 10 years. This also proves that my eyes have been healthy for a long time.

Finally, after this incident, I realized that eyes are really important. Try not to stay up late and not overuse them.
